    How to Play

    Stack 10 Pokémon on the ladder by National Pokédex number — lowest at the bottom, highest at the top. Use the slider above the version number to switch modes before you start.

    Mode 1

    You have 60 seconds and 3 skips. A Pokémon appears in the reveal window — tap an empty ladder slot to place it. To skip, tap the Skips button beside the reveal window. No repeats. When you’re out of skips, you must place the Pokémon shown.

    Once all 10 slots are filled, the timer resets to 20 seconds so you can rearrange them. Drag Pokémon, tap two to swap, or use the up/down arrows. Tap Lock In when ready, or the ladder locks automatically when time runs out.

    Mode 2

    When the game starts, all 10 slots fill automatically with random Pokémon. After the ladder is full, a 60-second timer begins.

    Reorder the stack with drag, tap-to-swap, or the up/down arrows. You also get 2 replaces: select a Pokémon on the ladder, then tap Replace to swap it for a new one. Tap Lock In when you are done, or the board will lock in automatically when time runs out.

    Scoring

    Each Pokémon in the correct slot scores a flat 100. A shiny always adds +50 (shown in gold). If that shiny is also in the correct slot, the whole slot is doubled to 300.

    Streak bonus: Your longest run of consecutive correct slots adds a multiplier — 10% for a streak of 2, then +5% per extra Pokémon. A perfect board of 10 always gets a 100% streak bonus. The streak applies after time bonus is added.

    Time bonus: Mode 1 awards points equal to the seconds left when you finished placing all 10. Mode 2 awards points equal to the seconds left when you lock in (so locking in early earns a bigger bonus). In both modes, you need at least one Pokémon in the correct slot to receive any time bonus.

    Shinies: Each placed Pokémon has a 1/100 chance to be shiny (revealed at the end).

    How to Play

    Stack 10 Pokémon on the ladder by National Pokédex number — lowest at the bottom, highest at the top.

    You have 60 seconds and 3 skips. Tap a slot to place the current Pokémon. To skip, tap the Skips button beside it. No repeats. When you’re out of skips, you must place the Pokémon shown.

    Once all 10 slots are filled, the timer resets to 15 seconds so you can rearrange. Drag Pokémon to reorder, or tap two to swap. Lock in when ready — or the ladder locks automatically when time runs out.

    Scoring: Each Pokémon in the correct slot scores a flat 100. A shiny always adds +50 (shown in gold). If that shiny is also in the correct slot, the whole slot is doubled to 300.

    Streak bonus: Your longest run of consecutive correct slots adds a multiplier — 10% for a streak of 2, then +5% per extra Pokémon. A perfect board of 10 always gets a 100% streak bonus. The streak applies after time bonus is added.

    Time bonus: You also score points equal to the seconds left on the clock when you finished placing all 10.

    Shinies: Each placed Pokémon has a 1/4096 chance to be shiny (revealed at the end).
